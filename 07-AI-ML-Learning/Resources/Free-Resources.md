### Machine Learning Fundamentals

**Andrew Ng's Machine Learning (Coursera)**
- **Link**: [Coursera ML Course](https://www.coursera.org/learn/machine-learning)
- **Duration**: 60 hours
- **Level**: Beginner to Intermediate
- **Topics**: Supervised/Unsupervised Learning, Neural Networks
- **Why**: THE foundational course. Start here.
- **Certification**: Free to audit, $49 for certificate

**Google's ML Crash Course**
- **Link**: [Google ML Crash Course](https://developers.google.com/machine-learning/crash-course)
- **Duration**: 15 hours
- **Level**: Beginner
- **Topics**: ML fundamentals with TensorFlow
- **Why**: Quick introduction with interactive exercises
- **Certification**: Free completion certificate

**Fast.ai: Practical Deep Learning**
- **Link**: [Fast.ai Course](https://course.fast.ai/)
- **Duration**: 30 hours
- **Level**: Beginner to Advanced
- **Topics**: Deep Learning, Computer Vision, NLP
- **Why**: Best hands-on approach, top-down learning
- **Certification**: Free

**Stanford CS229: Machine Learning**
- **Link**: [YouTube Playlist](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLoROMvodv4rMiGQp3WXShtMGgzqpfVfbU)
- **Duration**: 40 hours
- **Level**: Intermediate to Advanced
- **Topics**: Mathematical foundations, algorithms
- **Why**: Deep theoretical understanding

We'll go topic-wise with curated medium-to-hard problems (LeetCode-style), including variations to mimic follow-up rounds:

| Topic                        | Key Focus Areas                                | Sample Problems                                      |
| ---------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------- |
| **Arrays & Strings**         | Sliding window, two-pointer, prefix/suffix sum | Max subarray sum, Longest substring without repeat   |
